ABOUT

First Laurel Logistics Service is a logistics company based in Calabar Cross River State, We pick up parcels from its point of origin and deliver to various locations within Calabar and environs.

With our online service you can request for dispatch riders directly from your phone and keep tabs on your delivery progress.
