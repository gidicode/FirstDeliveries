ABOUT FIRST LAUREL LOGISTICS SERVICE (FLLS)

First Laurel Logistics Service is a courier service based in Calabar Cross River State, We pick up parcel from its point of origin and deliver to various various locations around Calabar.

We aslo provide In-shopping service (going shopping for people) that is reliable and the quality of item is checked before purchase, Items are gotten from the best stores around town. With our online service you can request for dispatch riders directly from your phone and keep tabs on your delivery progress.

This service is very favorable to vendors who want to keep track of their product deliveries to various Customers they send deliveries to. With Over 20 dispatch bikes, tricycle and vechiles we are ready to serve all your delivery needs from parcl to heavy duty equipment.

You can make payment to us with Cash, Bank Transfer, Card payments online.
