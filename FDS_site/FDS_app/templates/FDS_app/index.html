SHOPPING REQUEST INFORMATION

With our Shopping Service we save you the time and stress needed to carter for some of your shopping needs, be it in the market or store.

To place a Shopping Request, You have to create an account, log into your dashboard and create a Shopping request
Transfer the estimated amount for purchase to our account number displayed on the green notifcation, Or check for our account number on your menu bar.
When we confirm payment, we access the shopping list submitted to determine the space it would occupy, If all items can fit in the bike bucket, the cost would be N500 else the cost is N1000.
We will first deduct the cost of delivery and the purchase service fee which is N600 , before going to purchase your items.
Note:
If the cost of the items at the market is more than the money at hand, you will be contacted to for further decision, if you have a balance left from the purchase, it will be returned to you or you choose to leave it in your wallet with us for future purchase.
