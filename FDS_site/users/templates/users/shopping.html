SHOPPING REQUEST INFORMATION

With our Shopping Service we save you the time and stress needed to carter for some of your shopping needs, be it in the market or store.

To place a Shopping Request, You have to create an account, log into your dashboard and create a Shopping request

Make payments with your card or through bank transfer

We access the shopping list submitted and go for purchase to deliver to address given

We will first deduct a delivery fee of N500 or N1000 and a purchase service fee off N500 or N700, before going to purchase your items.

Note:
If the cost of the items at the market is more than the estimated amount paid, you will be contacted to for further decision taking. If you have a balance left from the purchase, it will be returned to you on delivering your items.
